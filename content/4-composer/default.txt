Title: Composer

----

Subtitle: 

----

Subtitle-2: I started composing as a student during my college years in Los Angeles while following my courses of ear-training,harmony and counterpoint at the Los Angeles Pierce College and private composition and orchestration courses. I went through the different aspects of 20th century compositional techniques but it took me several years to find my own language and, once this happened, started composing on a regular basis with a personal style: very close to latin american popular music (but always rich in harmony and rhythm), jazz and European "serious" music from the first half of the 20th century. Through all these years of living in Europe -since 1974- I have used this combination of influences to express myself in many types of musical situations (chamber music, film and tv commercials,big band arrangements, music for the theatre and work commissions from my fellow colleagues at the Conservatoire Populaire de Musique in Geneva.
